# <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 240)">What's it is:</span>
The 「が」keyword is an<span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)"> <b>identifier particle</b></span>, think of it like it always answer a "slient" question:
<div align="center"> Who is something </div>
Or maybe you could think of it "points a finger" at the noun to indentify it.
# <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 240)">Some examples:</span> 
*  私が学生。
The   「が」 here indentify 私 as a 学生
* 私はワインが好きです
The  『が」here indentify　ワイン: As for me, the thing i like is wine
